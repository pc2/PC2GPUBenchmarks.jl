Tesla V100-SXM2-32GB (sm_70, 32.00 GiB)

Benchmarks were run on the GPU cluster of the physics faculty of University of Bielefeld.

For comparison: Datasheet by NVIDIA.

Peakflops

Theoretical

julia> theoretical_peakflops_gpu(; dtype=Float32);
Theoretical Peakflops (TFLOP/s):
 └ max: 15.7

julia> theoretical_peakflops_gpu(; dtype=Float64);
Theoretical Peakflops (TFLOP/s):
 └ max: 7.8

 julia> theoretical_peakflops_gpu_tensorcores(; dtype=Float16);
Theoretical Peakflops (TFLOP/s):
 └ max: 125.3

Empirical

julia> peakflops_gpu_fmas(; dtype=Float32);

 └ max: 15.47
